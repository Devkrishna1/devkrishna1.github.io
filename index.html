About

Hello! I'm a dynamic and detail-oriented Data Analyst with a rich foundation in statistical analysis, programming, and data visualization. Currently pursuing an M.Sc. in Data Analytics at Aston University, I am passionate about leveraging data to uncover insights that drive strategic decisions and process improvements.

My journey began with a B.Tech in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Rajagiri School of Engineering and Technology, where I graduated with a commendable CGPA of 7.74. After graduation, I embarked on a professional path at Ford Motor Private Limited in Chennai, swiftly advancing from a support role to become the anchor of a development team. My tenure at Ford was marked by significant achievements, including developing key features that drastically improved engagement and conversion rates, as well as leading major initiatives that enhanced our E-Commerce platform's user experience and system efficiency.

Beyond my professional life, I'm deeply engaged in a variety of extracurricular activities that keep me inspired and balanced. I am an enthusiastic sports fan, following Cricket, Football, and Formula 1 passionately. Additionally, I enjoy playing video games, which not only serve as a great leisure activity but also help in honing my strategic thinking and problem-solving skills.

I am currently involved in a project for my M.Sc. dissertation where I am developing an integrated dashboard for sustainable investment analysis, reflecting my commitment to applying data analytics in addressing real-world challenges.

With a strong foundation in technical skills and a collaborative spirit, I am eager to contribute to impactful data-driven projects and continue my learning journey in the ever-evolving field of data analytics.
